Automatic Teller Machine

Whether you want to withdraw cash, deposit cash or undergo any important fund transfers immediately, it is not exactly convenient to always visit the bank branch and wait in long queues to perform such quick and easy transactions. This is where an Automated Teller Machine or an ATM comes into play.

These machines allow you to undergo self-serviced transactions without the help of an actual teller and without having to visit the bank branch. Automated teller machines or ATMs allow many transactions, including cash withdrawals, cash deposits, and fund transfers.
